About

Barbo's Better Bookmark Organizer v1.0.0

Organize, tag, and maintain your Firefox bookmarks efficiently.

Features:

  • Review random bookmarks to keep your collection organized
  • Find and remove duplicate bookmarks
  • Identify old/unused bookmarks
  • Tag and categorize bookmarks
  • Track bookmark usage statistics
  • Export and import bookmark data
