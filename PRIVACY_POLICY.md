# Privacy Policy for Barbo's Better Bookmark Organizer

**Last Updated**: November 2, 2024

## Overview

Barbo's Better Bookmark Organizer ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the Extension handles your data.

## Data Collection and Storage

### What Data is Stored

The Extension stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

1. **Bookmark Metadata**:
   - Custom tags you add to bookmarks
   - Last accessed timestamp for each bookmark
   - Access count (number of times you've visited a bookmark)
   - Review dates and counts
   - Archive status and dates
   - Original folder locations for archived bookmarks

2. **User Settings**:
   - Old bookmark threshold (days)
   - Review interval and count preferences
   - Archive retention period
   - Auto-scan and confirmation preferences
   - Theme preference

3. **Dismissed Similar Groups**:
   - Lists of bookmark ID combinations you've dismissed from the Similar Bookmarks view

### Where Data is Stored

All data is stored **locally in your browser** using Firefox's `browser.storage.local` API. No data is ever transmitted to external servers, third parties, or the extension developer.

## Data Usage

The Extension uses the stored data exclusively for:
- Organizing and categorizing your bookmarks
- Tracking which bookmarks you access
- Providing review queue functionality
- Detecting duplicate and similar bookmarks
- Managing archived bookmarks
- Maintaining your custom settings

## Data Sharing

**The Extension does NOT**:
- Collect any personal information
- Send any data to external servers
- Share data with third parties
- Track your browsing activity beyond bookmarked pages
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Display advertisements

## Permissions Explanation

The Extension requires the following Firefox permissions:

- **bookmarks**: To read and modify your bookmarks (core functionality)
- **storage**: To save metadata and settings locally in your browser
- **tabs**: To detect when you open bookmarked pages for usage tracking
- **history**: To access visit dates for bookmarks (used in usage tracking)

All permissions are used exclusively for the Extension's stated functionality.

## Data Control

You have complete control over your data:

- All data is stored locally on your device
- You can export your data at any time using the "Export Data" feature
- Uninstalling the Extension will remove all stored data
- You can manually clear data through Firefox's extension storage management

## Data Retention

- Bookmark metadata is retained as long as the Extension is installed
- Archived bookmarks are automatically deleted after your configured retention period (default: 90 days)
- All data is immediately deleted when you uninstall the Extension
